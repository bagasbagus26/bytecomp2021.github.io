About One-Health

One-Health is an integrated health service facility that was established in 1999. It is a hospital with 200 rooms and there are inpatient services with a capacity of 60 patients, whose capacity will be increased in line with the availability of supporting resources. In addition, emergency Installation facilities and laboratories are also opened which are open 24 hours. One Health is an innovative global approach with the goal of strengthening collaboration and communication in all aspects of human, animal and environmental health. One Health's core concept is Communication, Coordination, Collaboration that we embody into One Health.
